Baked Salmon

  • Pre-heat oven to 425 F, 220 C

  • Internal temp of salmon: 125 - 135 F, 51 - 57 C

  • Tin foil
  • Olive oil
  • Fish skin down
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice

  • Lemon slices

  • Leave in the oven for about 12 min, until internal temp is 125-135 F, 51-57 C

Roasted Potatoes and White Fish (Cod)

  • White fish (cod)
  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Rosemary
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Water

  • Lemon

  • Pre-heat oven to 400 F

  • Internal temp of cod: 145

  • Cover sheet pan with aluminum foil
  • Slice potatoes 1/8 inch
  • Mix potatoes with olive oil, rosemary, black pepper, and salt
  • Add a bit of water
  • Cover sheet pan
  • In the oven for about 20 minutes until potatoes are soft
  • Turn off oven and turn on broiler
  • Uncover potatoes
  • Put the fish on top of potatoes, adding oil and seasoning
  • Broil for about 10 minutes until internal temp is around 145
  • Add lemon juice and enjoy

Beef Shank pressure cooker

  • 2 pounds beef shank - in cubes, keep the bone
  • onion
  • garlic
  • light beer (pilsner, lager)
  • salt
  • potatoes

  • carrots

  • Saute onion and garlic
  • Add beef shank and bone
  • Cook and stir until the beef has released its water and the water evaporates (takes a while, make sure not to burn the bottom of the pan)
  • Add the beer, and fill with water until it covers a bit more than the beef
  • Add salt
  • Stir and close the lid
  • Once it gets pressure, turn down heat and let it cook for 20 minutes
  • Take the pressure off, add the potatoes and carrots and let it cook until they are ready
    • If there’s not enough liquid, add some more water and close the lid again, letting it cook after pressure is resumed for about 5 minutes
    • If there’s too much water, don’t close the lid.

Serve with rice, beans, farofa, collard greens, etc.
